Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking 2–3 days in advance to ensure your preferred time slot. That said, we often have same-week availability — especially mid-week. For ceramic coatings or paint correction, we suggest a week's notice since those appointments run longer.

We carry our own water supply and power for most services, so no hookup is needed for standard details. For larger jobs like ceramic coating or steam cleaning, we may request access to a standard outdoor spigot and a regular electrical outlet — we'll let you know at booking time.

A Basic Shine takes 1.5–2 hours. A Standard Detail typically runs 3–4 hours. Our Premium Package, which includes paint correction and ceramic coating, can take 6–8 hours. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book based on your specific vehicle.

We cover all of Greenville and the broader Upstate region — including Simpsonville, Greer, Mauldin, Taylors, Fountain Inn, Travelers Rest, Duncan, Easley, Anderson, and Spartanburg. If you're not sure, just ask. We're always looking to expand and may still be able to reach you.
