Cold Call Script

Straight Line Method  ·  Screen share close

The Script
Phase 1 Introduction ~30 sec

"Hey, is this [Owner Name]?"

Pause: 1 sec — let them answer

"Hey man, it's Almir."

Pause: 0.5 sec

"My mom's friend Christina told me about you guys, so I took a look at the business."

Pause: 1 sec

"You guys seem to be doing great work. Your Google reviews show people already trust you."

Pause: 1 sec

"Only thing I noticed is, I couldn't find a website for you guys. Is that right?"

Pause: 2 sec — shut up and let them answer
Phase 2 Body ~60 sec
If they say yes, no website:

"That's actually why I'm calling."

Pause: 0.5 sec

"I just recently graduated college and started my own web design agency…"

Pause: 0.5 sec

"and I actually built you guys a website."

Pause: 1.5 sec — let that land

"It's built around your business, the areas you serve, and the kind of work you do."

Pause: 1 sec

"The idea is simple."

Pause: 0.5 sec

"When someone hears about you from word of mouth, a lot of times they'll look you up before they call."

Pause: 1 sec

"Your Google page might show up…"

Pause: 0.5 sec

"but without a website connected to it, some people may not feel fully sold yet."

Pause: 1 sec

"The website gives them one clean place to see your work, see what you offer, and either call you or request a quote."

Pause: 1 sec

"Does that make sense?"

Pause: 2 sec — let them answer
Phase 3 Word-of-Mouth Tie Down ~30 sec

"And I'm assuming most of your work is probably word of mouth right now, right?"

Pause: 2 sec — let them answer
If they say yes:

"Perfect, man. That's usually the best type of business."

Pause: 0.5 sec

"The website doesn't replace word of mouth. It supports it."

Pause: 1 sec

"So when someone hears your name and looks you up, they don't hesitate or end up calling somebody else."

Pause: 1 sec

"Does that make sense?"

Pause: 1.5–2 sec
Phase 4 Close ~20 sec

"Since I already built you a website, I wanted to show it to you on a quick screen share."

Pause: 0.5 sec

"It takes about 10 minutes."

Pause: 0.5 sec

"You can look it over, see what your business would look like with a website, and then decide if it even makes sense."

Pause: 1 sec

"Would later today or tomorrow work better for you?"

Pause: 3 sec — shut up, do not talk first
Delivery Rules
Tonality
calm  ·  casual  ·  direct  ·  slightly curious
Not excited. Not needy. Not "sales guy." Sound like:
"I built something. It might help. Let me show you."
Pause Rules
After asking a direct question 2–3 sec
After saying "I built you guys a website" 1.5 sec
After explaining a value point 1 sec
Before asking for the appointment 1 sec
After the close 3 sec min
The biggest mistake: talking again after the close. Ask the question and shut up.
Words to Emphasize
Slow down slightly on these. Don't overact — just let them land.
built website word of mouth look you up request a quote 10 minutes later today or tomorrow

Make it sound like a normal conversation — not a script.

Your goal right now is not memorization.

Ask the question. Stop talking.
