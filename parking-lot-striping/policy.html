ADA Compliance Statement

All ADA markings applied by StripeRight Parking Solutions are executed in strict accordance with:

  • ADA Standards for Accessible Design (2010 ADAS) — including ADAAG Chapter 5 (Parking Spaces)
  • South Carolina Accessibility Code — as adopted and enforced by the SC Building Codes Council
  • DOJ Enforcement Guidelines — following Department of Justice technical assistance publications on parking facility requirements

We provide written confirmation of ADA-specification compliance upon project completion upon request. For properties undergoing DOJ review or accessibility audits, we are available to provide supporting documentation.
